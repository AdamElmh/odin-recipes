Cheesy ramen

Descrition

Melting cheese in ramen makes for an extremely simple and delicious spin on typical ramen noodles that can be easily made in the college environment.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Bring water to a boiling point in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2minues.
  3. step 3: Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and packet and cheese until well blended.
